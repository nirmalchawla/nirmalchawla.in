About

The Story Behind the Strategy

I never planned a linear career — and that's exactly what makes my perspective valuable.

My journey started at NIT Rourkela, where I built the engineering foundation that still shapes how I think about systems and scale. But I knew I wanted to be closer to where business decisions are made — so I pursued my PGDM at IIM Ahmedabad, one of the most transformative experiences of my life.

From there, I've worked across continents and industries — insurance and banking at Cognizant Consulting and PwC, telecom and media at Airtel, sports business at Rajasthan Royals, and technology at HP. Each chapter taught me something different about growth, transformation, and the human side of strategy.

Today, I sit at the intersection of AI strategy and business growth. I help organisations move beyond the AI hype — building practical roadmaps, identifying high-impact use cases, and turning digital ambition into measurable outcomes. I'm certified in AI product leadership via HP and Duke University, and I bring a builder's mindset to everything I do.

Whether it's launching India's first SMB Experience Centres at HP, moving manufacturing to India during Covid at Airtel, or identifying a $200Mn opportunity in cricket — I lead with impact numbers, not job titles.
